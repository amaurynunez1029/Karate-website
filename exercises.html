Makiwara:
The makiwara is a age old symbol of karate. It is used to toughen up the hands. Punches, and similar strikes became much more powerful after proper use as instructed by a sensei. The makiwar is much like the punching bag of today except it is a much tougher surface to endure. Only after you have developed a good punching form should you start using this tool.

Weapons:
Weapons such at the nunchuks and the bo staff offer a new view to training which will help you become a more skilled fighter. Usually these are only introduced once you have mastered a few of the basics.

Agility and Combat Training:
This is the core of traingin where new techiniques maybe introduced. Movement, Strikes, Blocks, Situaltional awareness might be some of the things you may encounter in these classes.
